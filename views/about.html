about

On January 1st 2020 I started my painting journey, taking oil painting classes at Schilderles Amsterdam.

At some point I started experimenting with digital painting. It made it much easier to get started, and it helped me get into a painting routine.

I realized that I wanted to share my work with other artists, be part of a creative community. So I started sharing my work on instagram. I used the handle @creatievelingetje.paintings to share it anonymously at first. I enjoyed this more than I thought I would, and it motivated me to practice painting regularly.

I do this next to my job as a software developer. It's mostly a creative outlet for me.

Follow me on Instagram @creatievelingetje.paintings to follow me on this creative journey. You can view my Instagram as my sketchbook. I will show work in progress and more experimental pieces.

Shoot me a message through instagram or an email if you have any questions.

the blue hat

this is my remake of "the blue hat" or "het blauwe hoedje" from kees van dongen. sometimes it can be hard to pick a subject to paint. in those cases, i like studying one of my favorite paintings.
