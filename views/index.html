portrait painting

capturing the likeness of a being in a painting is a wonderful challenge to me. i keep coming back to painting portraits. portrait painting is hard, but rewarding. it is thrilling to see the face come to life.

traditional painting & digital painting

i work with traditional canvasses and oil paint, and sometimes acrylic paint. next to that i paint digitally. digital painting has opened up a whole world of possiblities for me, but mostly, i paint more often and stick to a routine.
