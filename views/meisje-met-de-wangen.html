i completely screwed up one eye, it was too high, it was soggy, dark, jagged. this was a nightmare! i painted over it with light colors, let it dry, and repainted the eye. i learned to work with colors, to go easy with dark colors, that a mistake is fine, you can correct it. that parts can take a long time but can be rewarding, such as hands.
